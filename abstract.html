    Lampiran Presentation!!

Integrated Learning is an activity that must be carried out and is one of the graduation requirements for all 9th-grade Santa Ursula Junior High School Students in the 2023/2024 academic year. One of the activities of Integrated Learning is the implementation of a bazaar which took place on 4th-7th of December 2023 and 11th of December 2023. This bazaar activity is carried out to train female students' skills in business and entrepreneurship, starting from making advertisements, determining goods and prices, bazaar design, calculation and distribution of profits, and even the preparation of financial reports. Apart from the bazaar, we are also trained to make environmentally friendly food ingredients, namely through biotechnology activities.

Various Challenges

Various challenges were experienced by us during the process so far. One of them is when we process together in making biotechnology products. Even though the manufacturing process went very smoothly, where we had studied and understood each manufacturing step well and implemented it as well, it turned out that our biotechnology yoghurt product failed, namely it could not thicken. From here, we tried to find the cause of the failure so that when we were given the opportunity to make yoghurt again in biotechnology practice later, we didn't repeat the same mistakes, which could make our yoghurt fail. It turns out, our mistake was that when the temperature of the milk was still quite hot, we had put yoghurt as a source of good bacteria which would later develop through the incubation process into the milk. The bacteria in the yogurt are also killed by this process, preventing them from growing. So, the yoghurt cannot thicken like what happened to our group's yoghurt. Apart from these challenges, we also experienced difficulties in preparing financial reports from bazaar sales and creating the Integrated Learning website. This is also because previously, we had never prepared financial reports or created a website using the CSS and Javascript programming language. So, we encountered various difficulties, such as not finding the formula in the calculations, our website not being able to run, and so on. However, all these difficulties were continued to be addressed by seeking assistance from teachers in that field. In this way, we gain enlightenment and succeed in overcoming all existing difficulties.

Through the various processes that we have gone through so far, starting from making biotechnology products, preparing and implementing the bazaar, to preparing reports on the results of the bazaar, we feel that even though there are various challenges in carrying it out, for example, we failed in the process of making cheese. However, thanks to cooperation and team spirit, we were able to overcome various difficulties and do everything together. So, in accordance with Serviam's character value "Team Unity Spirit", we realize that with group cooperation, we will be able to get through all the difficult things and problems during this process well.
