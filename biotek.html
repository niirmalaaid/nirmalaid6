Bagaimana Cara Membuat Keju dan Yoghurt?



Keju merupakan sebuah makanan yang dihasilkan dengan memisahkan zat-zat padat dalam susu melalui proses pengentalan/koagulasi. Proses pengentalan ini dilakukan dengan bantuan bakteri Lactococcus lactis dan Lactobacillus bulgaricus. Bakteri lactococcus ini berperan untuk menurunkan pH. Jika pH turun, maka akan membantu proses pertumbuhan dari bakteri lactobacillus bulgaricus. Bakteri lactobacillus sendiri mempunyai peran penting, yaitu untuk menggumpalkan protein-protein yang ada di dalam susu tadi.

Bahan:
  • 1 liter susu murni
  • 3 sendok makan cuka
  • 3 sendok teh garam
Alat:
  • Panci
  • Sendok teh & sendok makan, untuk menakar banyaknya bahan yang digunakan, seperti garam dan cuka
  • Toples
  • Pengaduk
  • Kain bersih untuk menyaring

Proses membuat keju beserta durasinya:


No. Proses Pembuatan Durasi Pengerjaan
1 Masukkan susu ke panci, aduk terus hingga mendidih. Tambahkan cuka untuk memisahkan zat padat pada susu. Aduk rata. 15 Menit
2 Setelah gumpalan susu terpisah, siapkan saringan berupa kain bersih untuk menyaring bakal keju. Saring hingga benar-benar terpisah dari airnya.Proses saring dilakukan sambil ditekan secara perlahan-lahan. Campur hasil perasan (gumpalan keju) dengan garam secukupnya (3 sendok teh). 10 Menit
3 Gumpalan keju disimpan di dalam toples dan didiamkan di dalam kulkas dengan suhu 5-10 derajat Celcius. 10 Menit
Keju yang sudah padat dapat langsung disantap maupun diolah menjadi hidangan lain
Total waktu pembuatan keju adalah : 25 menit + 24 jam proses pendinginan


Selain keju, kami juga membuat produk bioteknologi lainnya yaitu yoghurt. Yoghurt merupakan salah satu olahan susu yang diproses melalui proses fermentasi dengan penambahan kultur organisme yang baik, salah satunya yaitu bakteri asam laktat. Cara membuat yoghurt adalah memasukkan bakteri spesifik ke dalam susu di bawah temperatur yang dikontrol dan kondisi lingkungan. Fermentasi gula susu atau laktosa akan menghasilkan asam laktat yang berperan dalam protein susu. Proses ini menghasilkan tekstur seperti gel yang mengental lembut dan memberikan aroma khas pada yoghurt.

Bahan:
  • 1000 ml susu segar (whole milk atau skim milk)
  • 2 sdm plain yoghurt (yoghurt dengan kandungan bakteri probiotik atau bakteri yang masih hidup)
Bahan:
  • Toples besar dengan tutup
  • Panci
  • Pengaduk
  • Termometer makanan
  • Sendok makan

Proses membuat yoghurt beserta durasi pembuatannya:

No. Proses Pembuatan Durasi Pengerjaan
1 Tuang 1000 ml susu segar ke dalam panci dan panaskan dengan api kecil sambil diaduk perlahan. Pada saat proses pemanasan pastikan susu jangan sampai mendidih dan hangus pada bagian bawah. 20 Menit
2 Setelah susu sudah cukup panas, angkat susu dan dinginkan sampai suhu 43°C 15 Menit
3 Setelah mencapai suhu 43°C, tuang 2 sdm yogurt plain ke dalam panci yang berisi susu, lalu aduk rata 3 Menit
4 Pindahkan susu yang sudah tercampur dengan yogurt plain ke dalam toples dan tutup rapat. Diamkan yogurt pada suhu 38°C. 7 jam
5 Setelah didiamkan selama 7 jam atau lebih, dinginkan yogurt yang telah mengental ke dalam lemari es beberapa jam sebelum dikonsumsi 1 jam

Total waktu pembuatan: 38 menit + 8 jam didiamkan (7 jam proses inkubasi + 1 jam proses pendinginan)



Tambahan:

Proses inkubasi/pendiaman pada suhu 38 derajat celcius bisa dilakukan lebih lama. Semakin lama didiamkan, maka tingkat keasaman akan semakin tinggi dan tekstur yoghurt menjadi semakin kental.

How to Make Homemade Cheese and Yoghurt (Biotechnology Activity)

Do you know that we’re able to make our own cheese and yoghurt? We used to find these things in grocery shops, but now we’re going to explore the behind the scenes on how it works and how you can actually produce it by yourself. Without any further ado, let’s start it.

The process of separating the solids particle in milk through a coagulation has produced a food called cheese. This thickening process is carried out with the help of Lactococcus lactis and Lactobacillus bulgaricus bacteria. If the pH drops, it will help the growth process of Lactobacillus bulgaricus bacteria. Lactobacillus bacteria itself has an important role, which is to coagulate the proteins in the milk. Cheese is a food produced by separating the solids in milk through the process of curdling/coagulation. This thickening process is carried out with the help of Lactococcus lactis and Lactobacillus bulgaricus bacteria. If the pH drops, it will help the growth process of Lactobacillus bulgaricus bacteria. Lactobacillus bacteria itself has an important role to coagulate the proteins in the milk.

Tools we need to make cheese:
  • Pot
  • Teaspoons & tablespoons, to measure the amount of ingredients used, such as salt and vinegar
  • Jar
  • Stirrer
  • Clean cloth for straining
Ingredients:
  • 1 liter of whole milk
  • 3 tablespoons vinegar
  • 3 teaspoons of salt
Cheese-making process and duration:

First, the milk is put in a saucepan, it is stirred continuously until it boils. Second, vinegar is added to separate the solids in the milk. Stir well. 15 minutes. Third, once the milk clumps are separated, a strainer in the form of a clean cloth is prepared to strain the cheese. It is strained until completely separated from the water. Four, the straining process is done while pressing gently. Fifth, the strained product (cheese clumps) is mixed with enough salt (three teaspoons). 10 minutes. Next, the cheese clumps are stored in a jar and refrigerated at 5-10 degrees Celsius. 24 hours. Finally, the solidified cheese can be eaten immediately or processed into other dishes. The total amount of time to make this menu is 25 minutes with additional 24 hours refrigeration.

Apart from cheese, we also make another biotechnology product, which is yoghurt. Yoghurt is a type of dairy product that is processed through a fermentation process with the addition of good organism cultures, one of which is lactic acid bacteria. The way to make yoghurt is to introduce specific bacteria into milk under controlled temperatures and environmental conditions. Fermentation of milk sugar or lactose will produce lactic acid which plays a role in milk protein. This process produces a gel-like texture that thickens gently and gives the yoghurt a distinctive aroma.

Tools we need to make yoghurt:
  • Large jar with lid
  • Pot
  • Mixer
  • Food thermometer
  • Tablespoon
Ingredients:
  • 1000ml fresh milk (whole milk or skimmed milk)
  • 2 tbsp plain yoghurt (yoghurt containing probiotic bacteria or live bacteria)
First, 1000 ml of fresh milk is poured into a saucepan and heated over low heat while stirring gently. During the heating process, make sure that the milk does not boil and scorch at the bottom. 20 minutes. Next, the milk is removed when it is hot enough from the heat and cool to 43°C for 15 minutes. Once the milk has reached 43°C, 2 tbsp plain yoghurt is poured into the pan with the milk and stirred well around 3 minutes. Then, the milk mixed with plain yoghurt is transferred to a jar and the lid is closed tightly. Let the yoghurt stand at 38°C 7 hours. Finally, after standing for 7 hours or more, the thickened yoghurt is chilled in the fridge for a few hours before consuming it’s about 1 hour. We also have some additional information and tips on making it. The incubation/steeping process at 38 degrees centigrade can be done longer. The longer it sits, the higher the acidity level and the thicker the yoghurt texture. The total amount of time to make it is 38 minutes and 8 hours sitting (7 hours incubation process + 1 hour cooling process)

Chicken with Cheese Sauce


The cheese that we made as a result of this biotechnology process was then utilized as the main ingredient in the making of a craft dish that we also sold at the bazaar, namely chicken with cheese sauce. We only sold this dish offline in the bazaar, which was 18 servings with a price of Rp 25,000/size. Here is what we needed to make this dish:

Chicken Marination Ingredients:
  • 2kg chicken breast
  • 8 tbsp soya
  • 12 cloves of garlic
Flour Ingredients:
  • 20 tbsp crispy seasoning flour
  • 20 tbsp cornflour
  • 5 tbsp baking powder
  • 5 tsp black pepper
  • 8 eggs
Cheese Sauce Ingredients:
  • 2 packets cheese seasoning for fries
  • 25g cheese (bioengineered)
  • 4 boxes of kraft cheddar cheese
  • 4 boxes of 200ml liquid milk
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 3 tsp pepper
  • Boiled/mineral water to taste (to dilute the sauce if it's salty)
Tools we need to make chicken with cheese sauce:
  • Knife
  • Cutting board
  • Tablespoon
  • Teaspoon
  • Basin
  • Spatula
  • Frying pan
  • Stainer

Here is the process of making cheese sauce chicken: First, all the garlic is chopped finely. Second, all the spices are mixed and the chicken is marinated, let it sit for 30 minutes. Third, the egg is put in the chicken mixture, then it is stirred until smooth. Then, it is put in the flour mixture. Next, it is fried until golden brown. Remove, then set aside. Next, cheese mixture is mixed except corn flour and water. Stir until well blended and boil, then add corn flour. Finally, taste the flavor, if it's right, it can be removed from heat and it is ready to be served.
